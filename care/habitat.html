How to Set Up a Hamster's Enclosure

A complete guide on setting up a hamster's enclosure

Habitat / Setup

Hamsters need a safe and spacious environment with bedding, hides, climbing platforms, and wheels.

Diet

Provide a balanced diet with seed mixes, fresh vegetables, and occasional protein treats.

  • Seed mix: X% seeds, X% grains
  • Fresh vegetables: Carrots, cucumber, etc.
  • Protein treats: Mealworms, boiled egg, occasional

Health

Signs of good health, common diseases, and how to spot illness early.

  • Check eyes, teeth, and fur regularly
  • Monitor weight and activity
  • Veterinary checkups when needed

Enrichment & Play

Ideas for tunnels, toys, sand baths, and mental stimulation to prevent boredom.

  • Tunnels and tubes
  • Sand bath for cleaning
  • Chew toys for teeth health

Tips & Best Practices

  • Interact gently to build trust
  • Clean cage weekly without disturbing nesting too much
  • Rotate toys and hides for interest
